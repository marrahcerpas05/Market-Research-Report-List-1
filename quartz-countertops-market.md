<p><strong>The Quartz Countertops Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Press Molding</li><li>Casting Molding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The quartz countertops market features various types of production methods, primarily press molding and casting molding. Press molding involves compressing a mixture of quartz, resins, and pigments under high pressure to create dense, durable slabs. This process ensures uniform thickness and smooth finishes. In contrast, casting molding involves pouring the mixture into molds to form slabs, allowing for intricate designs and patterns. Both methods cater to different aesthetic preferences and functional requirements, making them popular choices for residential and commercial applications.</p></p>
