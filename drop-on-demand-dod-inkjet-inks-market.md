<p><strong>The Drop On Demand (DOD) Inkjet Inks Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermal DOD</li><li>Piezoelectric DOD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Drop On Demand (DOD) inkjet inks market is primarily categorized into two types: Thermal DOD and Piezoelectric DOD. Thermal DOD technology utilizes heat to create vapor bubbles that eject ink droplets, making it ideal for high-speed printing applications, particularly in consumer printers. In contrast, Piezoelectric DOD employs mechanical pressure from piezoelectric crystals to force ink out of the nozzle, allowing for precise droplet size control and compatibility with a wider range of ink types, including UV and solvent inks.</p></p>
