<p><p>The roofing primers market includes three main types: water-based primers, solvent-based primers, and other specialized formulations. Water-based primers are eco-friendly, easy to clean, and suitable for various roofing materials, offering good adhesion and quick drying times. Solvent-based primers, on the other hand, provide strong adhesion and durability, making them ideal for harsh environments, but require proper ventilation during application. Other market options may include specialty primers designed for specific substrates or unique conditions, enhancing overall roof performance and longevity.</p></p>
