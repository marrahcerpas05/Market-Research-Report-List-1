<p><p>The Non-Contact Dynamic Torque Sensor market is primarily divided into two types: sensors with bearings and bearingless sensors. Sensors with bearings utilize traditional mechanical bearings to support the rotating elements, providing accurate torque measurements while minimizing friction. In contrast, bearingless sensors eliminate mechanical bearings, using advanced technologies like electromagnetic or optical methods to measure torque. This design reduces wear and tear and enhances durability, making bearingless sensors ideal for high-performance applications where reliability and precision are critical.</p></p>
