<p><p>The Cross-Linked Polyethylene (PEX) market consists of various types, primarily High Density Polyethylene (HDPE) and Low Density Polyethylene (LDPE). HDPE features a high strength-to-density ratio, making it suitable for applications requiring durability, such as plumbing and gas piping. In contrast, LDPE, which is more flexible and has lower tensile strength, is commonly used in applications like insulation and packaging. Both types are crucial in diverse industries, including construction, automotive, and electrical, due to their unique properties and functionalities.</p></p>
