<p><strong>The Submarine Pipeline Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ERW Pipes</li><li>LSAW Pipes</li><li>SSAW Pipes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The submarine pipeline market comprises various pipe types used in offshore applications: </p><p>1. **ERW (Electric Resistance Welded) Pipes** are produced by welding rolled steel sheets, offering cost-effectiveness for shallow water installations.  </p><p>2. **LSAW (Longitudinal Submerged Arc Welded) Pipes** feature higher strength and resistance, making them suitable for deeper and more challenging environments.  </p><p>3. **SSAW (Spiral Submerged Arc Welded) Pipes** are characterized by their spiral welding process, providing flexibility for handling complex layouts and larger diameters, ideal for varied offshore scenarios.</p></p>
