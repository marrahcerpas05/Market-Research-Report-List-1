<p><p>The Non-contact Infrared Temperature Sensor market is primarily categorized into two types: Through Hole Thermopile Infrared Sensors and SMD (Surface Mount Device) Thermopile Infrared Sensors. Through Hole sensors are designed for traditional circuit boards, offering robust connections for high-temperature environments. In contrast, SMD sensors are compact and designed for modern electronic assemblies, allowing for automated production and space-saving applications. Both types facilitate accurate temperature measurements without direct contact, catering to various industries such as automotive, healthcare, and consumer electronics.</p></p>
