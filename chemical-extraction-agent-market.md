<p><p>The chemical extraction agent market includes three main types: neutral, acidic, and alkaline extractants. Neutral extractants are used for processes that require minimal reactivity, effectively isolating compounds without altering their state. Acidic extractants are employed to selectively extract metal ions or organic compounds, enhancing solubility through protonation. Alkaline extractants, on the other hand, promote extraction by neutralizing acidic components, facilitating the removal of specific substances. Each type serves distinct purposes depending on the nature of the target compounds and desired outcomes.</p></p>
