<p><strong>The Organic Virgin Olive Oil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Extra Virgin Olive Oil 0.8</li><li>Virgin Olive Oil 2.0</li><li>Lampante Olive Oil Above 2.0</li><li>Refined Olive Oil 0.3</li><li>Blended Olive Oil Composed Of 1.0</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The organic virgin olive oil market features several types based on acidity and processing methods. Extra virgin olive oil, with acidity levels below 0.8%, is the highest quality, retaining natural flavors and health benefits. Virgin olive oil has acidity up to 2.0%, offering good quality but slightly lesser characteristics. Lampante olive oil exceeds 2.0% acidity and is unsuitable for direct consumption. Refined olive oil, with acidity up to 0.3%, undergoes processing to remove defects. Blended olive oil combines refined and virgin oils, typically at 1.0% acidity, balancing cost and quality.</p></p>
