<p><p>Visual product configurator software can be categorized into four main types. Three-dimensional product configurators offer a more immersive experience by enabling users to manipulate 3D models. Virtual reality product configurators create fully immersive environments where users can interact with products in a simulated space. Augmented reality configurators enhance the real world with digital overlays, allowing users to visualize products in their actual environment.</p></p>
