<p><strong>The Circular Polarizer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Absorptive Polarizers</li><li>Beam-splitting Polarizers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The circular polarizer market is primarily divided into two types: absorptive polarizers and beam-splitting polarizers. Absorptive polarizers work by absorbing light waves oscillating in one orientation, allowing only specific polarized light to pass through. This type is commonly used in photography and display technologies for enhancing contrast. Beam-splitting polarizers, on the other hand, separate incoming light into two beams with different polarization states, making them essential in optical devices, such as projectors and scientific instruments, for improved image quality and efficiency.</p></p>
