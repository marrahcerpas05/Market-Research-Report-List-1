<p><p>The Continuus Level Monitor market comprises various types, each suited for specific applications. Differential Pressure monitors measure levels based on pressure differences in a liquid column. Guided Wave Radar utilizes microwave signals guided along a probe to determine level. Non-contact Radar employs electromagnetic waves to measure levels without physical contact. Ultrasonic sensors use sound waves for level detection, while Capacitance sensors measure changes in capacitance caused by level variations. Other technologies may include laser and float-based systems, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
