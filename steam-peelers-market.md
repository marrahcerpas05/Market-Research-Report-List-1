<p><p>Steam peelers are classified into fully automatic and semi-automatic types based on their operation and functionality. Fully automatic steam peelers handle the entire peeling process independently, requiring minimal human intervention. They are ideal for large-scale operations due to their efficiency and consistent performance. In contrast, semi-automatic steam peelers require some manual input, offering flexibility and lower investment costs.
