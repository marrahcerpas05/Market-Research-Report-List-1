<p><strong>The Rear View Camera Lens Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CCD Cameras</li><li>CMOS Cameras</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rear view camera lens market primarily includes two types: CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) cameras and CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) cameras. CCD cameras offer high image quality and low noise, making them ideal for low-light conditions. In contrast, CMOS cameras are energy-efficient, with faster processing speeds and integrated features, suitable for modern automotive applications. Both technologies enhance vehicle safety by providing clear rear visuals, aiding parking, and minimizing accidents, catering to the growing demand for advanced driver-assistance systems.</p></p>
