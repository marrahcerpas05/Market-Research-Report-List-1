<p><p>The ship unloader market is primarily divided into two types: stationary and mobile ship unloaders. Stationary ship unloaders are fixed installations that efficiently unload bulk materials from vessels at specific ports, ideal for high-capacity operations. In contrast, mobile ship unloaders are versatile and can be relocated between different terminals, making them suitable for smaller ports or varying cargo types. Both types cater to the growing demand for efficient handling of bulk goods in the logistics and shipping sectors.</p></p>
