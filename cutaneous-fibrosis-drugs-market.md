<p><p>The cutaneous fibrosis drugs market includes several key types targeting skin fibrosis. Corticosteroids are anti-inflammatory agents that reduce swelling and fibrosis by suppressing the immune response. Anti-fibrotic drugs aim to inhibit excessive collagen production and improve skin elasticity, effectively addressing fibrotic conditions. Immunoglobulins, derived from blood plasma, modulate the immune system and can help in autoimmune fibrotic diseases. Together, these drug classes play a crucial role in managing and treating various forms of cutaneous fibrosis to enhance patient quality of life.</p></p>
