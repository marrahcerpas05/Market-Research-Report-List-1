<p><p>Silicone room temperature glue is categorized mainly into two types: condensed type and addition type. </p><p>Condensed type silicone adhesive cures through a process that releases by-products, typically moisture, and forms strong bonds suitable for various applications. In contrast, addition type silicone adhesive cures through a chemical reaction without releasing by-products, offering superior strength and stability. Both types cater to diverse industries, including automotive and construction, each chosen based on specific bonding requirements and environmental conditions.</p></p>
