<p><p>The fire suppression agent market comprises various types: </p><p>1. **Water**: The most common and cost-effective agent, effective for many fire types but less suitable for electrical or flammable liquid fires. </p><p>2. **Clean Agents**: Non-toxic, environmentally friendly chemicals that extinguish fires without residue, ideal for sensitive environments. </p><p>3. **Foam**: Provides a blanket over flammable liquids, preventing re-ignition and controlling fire spread. </p><p>4. **Powder**: Suitable for a range of fires, particularly flammable solids and metals, effective but can leave residue that requires cleanup.</p></p>
