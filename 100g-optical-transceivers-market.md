<p><p>The 100G optical transceiver market comprises various form factors, each tailored for specific applications. SFF (Small Form Factor) and SFP (Small Form-Factor Pluggable) are compact designs for efficient data transmission. QSFP (Quad Small Form-Factor Pluggable) supports multiple channels, enhancing bandwidth. CFP (C Form-Factor Pluggable) offers high power and versatility for dense connections. XFP (10 Gigabit Small Form-Factor Pluggable) is optimized for 10G applications but can support higher rates. CXP (C Form-Factor Pluggable) is designed for very high-speed interconnections, suitable for data centers.</p></p>
