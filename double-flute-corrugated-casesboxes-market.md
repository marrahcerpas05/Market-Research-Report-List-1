<p><p>Double flute corrugated cases and boxes are categorized into three main types: slot type, nested type, and folding type. The slot type features pre-cut flaps that allow easy assembly, making it ideal for shipping and storage. Nested type boxes are designed to fit into each other, optimizing space and reducing shipping costs. Folding type boxes are assembled quickly and often have a flat design for efficient storage, providing versatility in packaging for various products. Each type caters to different logistics and storage needs.</p></p>
