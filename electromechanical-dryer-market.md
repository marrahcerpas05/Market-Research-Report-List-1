<p><ul><li>Electro Dryer</li><li>Mechanical Dryer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electromechanical dryer market comprises two primary types: electro dryers and mechanical dryers. Electro dryers utilize electrical energy to generate heat for the drying process, offering efficient energy consumption and faster drying times. In contrast, mechanical dryers rely on mechanical systems, such as rotating drums or conveyor belts, to facilitate airflow and moisture removal. Both types cater to various industries, including textiles, food processing, and pharmaceuticals, each chosen based on specific drying requirements, efficiency, and operational preferences.</p></p>
