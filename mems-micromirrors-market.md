<p><p>The MEMS micromirror market is primarily segmented into mono-axial and dual-axial MEMS mirrors. Mono-axial MEMS mirrors operate around a single axis, making them suitable for applications like projectors and barcode scanners, where limited motion is sufficient. In contrast, dual-axial MEMS mirrors allow movement along two axes, enabling more complex light manipulation essential for applications such as 3D imaging and advanced display technologies. The choice between the two types depends on the specific requirements of the intended application.</p></p>
