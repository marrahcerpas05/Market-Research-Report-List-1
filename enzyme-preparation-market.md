<p><p>The enzyme preparation market is categorized into six main types based on their biochemical functions. Oxidoreductases facilitate oxidation-reduction reactions, while Transferases are involved in transferring functional groups between molecules. Hydrolases catalyze hydrolysis reactions, breaking chemical bonds with water. Isomerases convert molecules into their isomers, altering their structure without changing their composition. Lyases remove groups from molecules without hydrolysis, and Ligases join two molecules together, often utilizing ATP. Each type plays a crucial role across various industries, including pharmaceuticals and biotechnology.</p></p>
