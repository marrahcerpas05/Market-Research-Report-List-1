<p><ul><li>Tapered Thread Type</li><li>Parallel Thread Type</li><li>MBT Type</li><li>Grout Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rebar coupler market encompasses various connection types for reinforcing steel bars. Tapered thread couplers use a conical shape to enhance grip, while parallel thread couplers feature uniform threads for straightforward alignment and secure connections. The MBT type is designed for mechanical bonding without welding, offering fast installation. Grout couplers utilize epoxy or cementitious materials to bond rebars, providing flexibility in construction. Each type addresses specific engineering requirements, contributing to structural integrity and efficiency in construction projects.</p></p>
