<p><p>The digital integrated circuit market comprises various types, each serving distinct functionalities. CPUs (Central Processing Units) are the primary processing units in computers, executing instructions and managing tasks. GPUs (Graphics Processing Units) specialize in rendering graphics and parallel computing. DSPs (Digital Signal Processors) handle real-time signal processing tasks. MCUs (Microcontrollers) integrate processing and control functions for embedded systems. Memory circuits store and retrieve data efficiently. Other markets include specialized ICs for communication, sensors, or automotive applications, broadening the circuit's utility.</p></p>
