<p><p>The acoustic materials market comprises various types, including ABS (Acrylonitrile Butadiene Styrene), fiberglass, polypropylene (PP), polyurethane (PU), and polyvinyl chloride (PVC). ABS offers durability and resistance to impact, making it suitable for soundproofing applications. Fiberglass provides excellent noise absorption and thermal insulation. PP is lightweight, versatile, and cost-effective for acoustic panels. PU is known for its flexibility and sound-dampening properties. PVC, being versatile and moisture-resistant, is used in various acoustic applications, providing sound isolation and reducing reverberation.</p></p>
