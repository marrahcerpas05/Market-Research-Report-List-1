<p><p>Electrofusion systems are utilized for joining thermoplastic pipes, with two primary market types based on the electrical waveform used: AC waveform and DC waveform. AC waveform systems typically offer easier integration with existing infrastructure and facilitate efficient heating through alternating current. In contrast, DC waveform systems provide more controlled heating, enhancing the welding process's precision and reducing energy consumption. Both types cater to various applications across industries, including water, gas, and wastewater management, emphasizing the need for reliable and efficient connections.</p></p>
